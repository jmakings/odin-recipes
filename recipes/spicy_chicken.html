Spicy Chicken

Recipes Homepage

Description

This is a yummy recipe to try if you and your family loves some healthy chicken for the gains, but also hates eating bland food. This recipe is very simple to make even if you're dumb like me.

Ingredients

  • Raw chicken (breast, thighs, or wings)
  • Trader Joes Siracha Barbeque Sauce
  • Black Pepper
  • Oregano
  • Paprika
  • Onion Salt
  • Old Bay Seasoning
  • Frank's Red Hot

Steps

  1. Thaw your chicken completely
  2. If chicken breast or thighs, cut into mid-sized pieces
  3. Place breasts in large bowl
  4. Combine the rest of the ingredients with the chicken in the bowl, mixing around to ensure all pieces of chicken are covered.
  5. Place chicken pieces in frying pan and cook on medium heat for 10-15 minutes
  6. Ensure that chicken is fully cooked, make sure none of the chicken is pink
  7. Serve with your choice of sides, goes great with vegetables and rice for maximum gains!
